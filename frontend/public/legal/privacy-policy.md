# Privacy Policy - VoiceLap

**Last Updated:** November 16, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to VoiceLap ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our voice AI platform and services.

By using VoiceLap, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information
We may collect the following personal information:
- **Account Information**: Name, email address, phone number
- **Payment Information**: Payment method details (processed securely through third-party payment processors)
- **Profile Information**: User preferences, settings, and profile data

### 2.2 Voice Data
- **Voice Recordings**: Audio files you upload or record for voice cloning
- **Voice Clones**: Generated voice models based on your recordings
- **Conversation Data**: Chat sessions and AI interactions

### 2.3 Usage Information
- **Device Information**: IP address, browser type, operating system
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform
- **Cookies and Tracking**: We use cookies to enhance user experience

### 2.4 Anonymous User Data
- **Temporary Sessions**: For users who use VoiceLap without creating an account
- **Session Data**: Automatically deleted after 24 hours

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Data Storage
- **Cloud Storage**: We use MongoDB Atlas for secure cloud database storage
- **Voice Files**: Stored securely with encryption
- **Backup**: Regular automated backups for data protection

### 4.2 Security Measures
- **Encryption**: Data encrypted in transit (SSL/TLS) and at rest
- **Access Control**: Role-based access to sensitive data
- **Authentication**: Secure JWT-based authentication
- **Monitoring**: Continuous security monitoring and logging

### 4.3 Data Retention
- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Cancelled Subscriptions**: Voice clones deleted 24 hours after cancellation
- **Anonymous Users**: All data deleted after 24 hours
- **Deleted Accounts**: Data permanently deleted within 30 days
